Trip to the South Pole

Russia's Attempt

Russian spaceship Luna-25 failed its mission in trying to land on the South Pole of the Moon. In the race to be the first, the ship flew erratically and crashed onto the surface. One of the main reasons for the voyage is the frozen water believed to be on that pole.

India's Attempt

On August 23rd, India made the first landing on the Moon's South Pole, racing against Russia. Chandrayaan-3, the unmanned moon lander, would be able to conduct tests on the Pole. This achievement is an important milestone for the country.

Launch of James Webb Telescope

In December 2021, the James Webb Telescope was launched from the reliable Ariane 5 rocket. One of the objectives of the telescope is to view the first galaxies created during the start of the universe.
